About the Author

Sundara Rudraiah

Sundara Rudraiah is a bilingual technology professional with over 30 years of global experience in software design, systems integration, and project leadership — including 22 years of work experience in Japan. A certified Project Management Professional (PMP), he holds multiple credentials in systems administration and database technologies, with extensive experience working on IBM, Oracle, Microsoft, Unix, and Linux platforms.

Fluent in Japanese and deeply attuned to cross-cultural collaboration, he previously served as director in multiple ventures. He stepped down from these roles after an ACL injury, shifting focus to create educational resources to empower others.

His blend of technical rigour and human insights shapes this book's compassionate mission — to support Indian caregivers with culturally relevant, visually clear, and academically sound guidance on dementia. Through thoughtful use of AI tools and a deeply personal connection to caregiving, Sundara Rudraiah bridges modern technology with empathetic storytelling — bringing clarity and care to the heart of every Indian home affected by dementia.
